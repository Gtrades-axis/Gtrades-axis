Complete Trading Curriculum

Follow a structured learning path designed to build professional traders from the ground up.

Module 01

Market Structure

Master trend identification, BOS, CHoCH, continuation, reversals, premium & discount, and institutional market narrative.

Module 02

Liquidity

Learn Buy Side Liquidity, Sell Side Liquidity, liquidity sweeps, inducement, equal highs & lows, and stop hunts.

Module 03

Supply & Demand

Identify institutional zones, fresh levels, mitigation, reaction zones, refinement and high probability entries.

Module 04

Trade Entries

Learn confirmations, lower timeframe execution, session timing, confluences and sniper entries.

Module 05

Risk Management

Position sizing, daily loss limits, risk-to-reward, partial profits, break-even and capital preservation.

Module 06

Trading Psychology

Develop discipline, emotional control, consistency, journaling, patience and a professional trader mindset.

Module 07

Trading Sessions

Master the Asian, London and New York sessions, session ranges and institutional timing.

Module 08

Fundamentals

Understand high-impact news, economic events, CPI, NFP, Interest Rates and how institutions react.

Module 09

Trading Tools

TradingView setup, MT5, trading journals, watchlists, alerts and workflow optimisation.
